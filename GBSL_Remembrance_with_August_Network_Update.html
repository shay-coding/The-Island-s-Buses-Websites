Frequently Asked Questions

Can I buy tickets in advance?

Yes! You can purchase day, weekly, and monthly tickets through our mobile app or at selected retailers. Single and return tickets can be bought onboard.

Is there a discount for under 19s?

Children under 5 travel free. Ages 5-15 pay child fares (50% discount). Students aged 16-25 receive discounts with valid ID.

Do tickets work across all routes?

Yes, all our day tickets, weekly passes, and monthly passes are valid on every GoBus South Coast route across the Isle of Wight.
