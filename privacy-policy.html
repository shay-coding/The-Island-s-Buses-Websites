Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Travel Information: Bus stops visited, travel routes, ticket purchases, and journey times.

  • Location Data: GPS data when using location-based services or mobile applications.
  • Customer Interactions: Records of communications with our customer service team.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To deliver and manage our bus services, including ticketing and customer support.
  • Communication: To send service updates, promotions, and other relevant information (you can opt out at any time).
  • Service Improvement: To analyse usage patterns and improve our services and customer experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, such as fraud prevention and regulatory requirements.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in operating our services (e.g., payment processors, IT support).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Restriction: Limit the processing of your data.
  • Portability: Obtain your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data for certain purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
